Athletics & Sport

Sport at His Church School

His Church School encourages all learners to participate in school sports as it not only promotes essential values such as teamwork, leadership, discipline, resilience, sportsmanship, physical fitness, time management, and respect for authority, but also instils Biblical values such as compassion, humility, integrity, and stewardship.

Integrating these values into sports activities fosters the holistic development of learners' character, guiding them to adopt Biblical values both on and off the sport field.

SACSSA affiliated: South African Christian Schools Sports Association.

KZNCSSA affiliated: KwaZulu-Natal Christian Schools Sports Association, North Durban Zone.

Sport Affiliations

Year Round

Sports by Term

Term 1

Swimming

Open to boys and girls between the ages of 6 and 19. Disciplines include freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly, and individual medley. Swimmers can qualify for Zonal, Provincial (KZNCSSA), and National (SACSSA) levels.

Mini Cricket

Open to boys and girls between the ages of 6 and 9. Two teams (U8 and U10) participate in the KZNCSSA Mini Cricket Festival.

Cricket

Open to boys between the ages of 10 and 19. Opportunity to participate in the KZNCSSA Provincial Cricket Festival with prospects of selection for KZNCSSA Teams.

Term 2

Mini Soccer

Open to boys and girls under the age of 9. A team participates in the Under 9 KZNCSSA Provincial Winter Sports.

Mini Netball

Open to girls under the age of 9. A team participates in the Under 9 KZNCSSA Provincial Winter Sports.

Netball

Girls between the ages of 10 and 19, if selected, take part in the KZNCSSA Provincial Winter Sports.

Soccer

Boys and girls between the ages of 10 and 19 have separate teams and, if selected, take part in the KZNCSSA Provincial Winter Sports.

Cross Country

Age groups Under 6 to Under 19. Runners can qualify for Zonal, Provincial (KZNCSSA), and National (SACSSA) levels.

Term 3

Athletics

Track and field disciplines including sprints, relays, long jump, high jump, shot put, javelin, and discus. Age groups Under 7 to Under 19. Athletes can qualify for Zonal, Provincial (KZNCSSA), and National (SACSSA) levels.

Term 4

Chess

Offered throughout the year with multiple online events. Contestants can participate in the KZNCSSA Provincial Chess Event and qualify for the SACSSA National Chess Event.

Table Tennis

Open to boys and girls between the ages of 6 and 19. Athletes can participate in the KZNCSSA Provincial Table Tennis, an individual-based trial for KZNCSSA Team selection.

Beyond The Classroom

Enrichment Programmes

Leadership Courses

His Church School offers leadership development programmes designed to equip learners with the skills, confidence, and Biblical foundations needed to lead effectively in their communities, schools, and future workplaces.

Firefighting

Selected staff and learners are trained in firefighting techniques, equipping them with practical skills for emergency response. This programme builds confidence, teamwork, and a sense of responsibility.

First Aid

His Church School provides First Aid training to equip learners and staff with life-saving skills. Participants receive Level One First Aid certification, preparing them to respond effectively in emergency situations.

Serving Others

Community Outreach

His Church School believes that true education extends beyond the classroom. Through community outreach initiatives, our learners are taught to serve others with compassion, humility, and love, reflecting the heart of Jesus Christ in their communities.

Outreach activities are integrated into the school calendar, giving learners practical opportunities to live out their faith and make a tangible difference in the lives of those around them.
